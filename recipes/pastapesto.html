Pasta with Pesto

Pasta with pesto is a classic dish that is loved by many for its simplicity and delicious flavor. Pesto, a sauce made from basil, pine nuts, garlic, parmesan cheese, and olive oil, is the perfect accompaniment to a variety of pasta shapes. Whether it's spaghetti, penne, or fusilli, the pasta provides a hearty base for the bright and fragrant pesto to shine.

This dish is quick and easy to make, making it a popular option for busy weeknights or when you want to whip up a comforting meal. To make pasta with pesto, simply cook the pasta according to the package instructions, then toss it with the pesto sauce in a large serving bowl. You can also add ingredients such as cherry tomatoes, grilled chicken, or saut√©ed mushrooms to make the dish even more filling. Serve the pasta hot, sprinkled with extra parmesan cheese and freshly cracked black pepper. Enjoy your delicious and flavorful plate of pasta with pesto!

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces of pasta of your choice (spaghetti, penne, fusilli, etc.)
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tablespoons pine nuts
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions in a large pot of salted water. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a food processor or blender, combine the basil leaves, pine nuts, garlic, parmesan cheese, and a pinch of salt. Pulse until finely chopped.
  3. With the motor running, slowly drizzle in the olive oil until the mixture becomes a smooth, creamy sauce.
  4. In a large serving bowl, toss the cooked pasta with the pesto sauce until the pasta is evenly coated.
  5. Season with additional salt and black pepper to taste, if desired.
  6. Serve hot and sprinkle with extra parmesan cheese on top, if desired. Enjoy!
