Tahini Miso Ramen

Description

Tahini Miso Ramen is a unique and flavorful twist on the traditional Japanese noodle dish. It combines the nutty and slightly bitter taste of tahini with the salty, savory flavors of miso to create a delicious broth that perfectly complements the noodles and toppings. The broth is made by cooking garlic, ginger, and green onions in oil, then adding a mixture of tahini, miso paste, and soy sauce to create a rich and umami-packed base.

This type of ramen is often topped with traditional ingredients such as sliced mushrooms, roasted seaweed, and green onions, as well as more unconventional toppings like boiled eggs, roasted garlic, and sesame seeds. The addition of tahini in the broth gives it a creamy texture and a nutty flavor, while the miso provides depth and complexity. Overall, Tahini Miso Ramen is a comforting and filling meal that is perfect for cold winter days or when you want to try something new and unique. Whether you are a fan of traditional ramen or are looking for a new flavor experience, Tahini Miso Ramen is definitely worth trying.

Ingredients

  • 4 portions of fresh ramen noodles
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 inch piece of ginger, minced
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1/4 cup tahini
  • 3 tablespoons miso paste
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 6 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
  • Toppings of your choice (e.g. boiled egg, sliced mushrooms, roasted seaweed, green onions, sesame seeds, etc.)
  1. Cook the ramen noodles according to package instructions and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the garlic, ginger, and green onions and cook until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
  3. In a small bowl, mix together the tahini, miso paste, and soy sauce until smooth.
  4. Add the tahini mixture to the pot and stir well. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  5. Gradually add the broth to the pot, whisking constantly to avoid lumps. Bring the broth to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Divide the cooked ramen noodles evenly among four bowls.
  7. Ladle the tahini miso broth over the noodles.
  8. Top with your desired toppings and serve immediately. Enjoy!
